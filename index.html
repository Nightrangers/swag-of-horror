The Whispers

A demo horror story in 40 chilling parts.

Part 1: The Whisper Begins

Sarah moved into a new apartment. Whispers warned her to leave. One night, she vanished. Only a note remained: "I'm waiting."

Part 2: The Disappearance

Police found her apartment empty. Walls carved with "I'm still here." More notes appeared. The whispers never stopped.

Part 40: The Final Whisper

Mirrors worldwide went black. Final message: "You opened the story. Now it belongs to you." The Whispers are done telling their story... now they want yours.
