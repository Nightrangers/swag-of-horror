The Horror Story: The Second Shadow

The room was quiet except for the ticking clock on the wall.

The lights were off, and the window was slightly open, letting in the cool night air.

At exactly 12:00 AM, the streetlight outside turned on.

A shadow stretched across the wall.

At first, nothing seemed wrong.

The shadow moved when Alex moved.

It stopped when Alex stopped.

Then something changed.

A second shadow slowly formed beside the first.

There was only one light.
Only one person.

Alex stood perfectly still.

One shadow copied the stillness.

The other did not.

It bent its head, as if noticing the room for the first time.

Its shape twitched, stretching longer than it should.

The air felt heavier.

Breathing sounded louder.

The streetlight flickered once.

When it turned back on, only one shadow remained on the wall.

The room felt normal again.
